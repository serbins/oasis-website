The Oasis Screen Clean Method

The Oasis Screen Renewer and Protectant Service transforms your dull, dry and dusty screens to look new again. This a great product that I really like. It’s a cleaner and protectant, all in one to renew the luster of your screens. It really works well and sometimes is the only solution I’ve found that cleans screens well enough.

How does it work, where does the dust go?

The special formula of emulsions, UV inhibitors, glossing agents, and dust repellents don’t just cover the dust, it destroys it. Dust particles are organically dissolved making the screens look like new, and the dust repellents help to keep them looking that way.

Does the screen contain any dangerous chemicals?

No. This product is completely safe and contains no solvents, phosphorus, or petroleum distillates that can cause ozone-depletion. It’s also non-toxic, non-flammable, inert and non-reactive.
